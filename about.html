Welcome to Bun Bun Bakeshop! We are just a couple of guys with a passion to bake, especially cinnamon rolls! We have grown up in Pittsburgh all our lives, and we are excited to be able to give back to our community.

We are a small bakery that specializes in cinnamon rolls, from our famous original cinnamon rolls to a variety of artisan flavors. Our shops are located in Pittsburgh, PA and we ship fresh rolls anywhere in the Pittsburgh area.
